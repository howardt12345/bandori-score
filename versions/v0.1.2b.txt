`v0.1.2b` Private beta is now merged into `main`

Changelog:
  Features:

    - Checks for full combo and all perfect in the score compare
  Fixes: 
    - Fixed an issue where compare would fail when adding a song for the first time
    - Improves OCR accuracy
    - Various minor bugfixes
